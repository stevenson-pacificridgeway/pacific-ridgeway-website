Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

Pacific Ridgeway Insurance Solutions, a DBA of Horizon Inc. ("Pacific Ridgeway," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our website or communicating with us, you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide to us directly, such as when you fill out a form, request a quote, or contact us. This may include your name, phone number, email address, mailing address, and details relevant to the insurance or financial products you are interested in. We also collect basic technical information automatically, such as your device type and how you use our website.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your questions and requests for quotes or services.
  • Contact you by phone, email, or text message about your inquiry, appointments, policies, and related services.
  • Provide, improve, and personalize our services.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

Text Messaging (SMS)

If you provide your phone number and consent, we may send you text messages related to your inquiry, appointments, policies, and customer service. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP, and you can reply HELP for assistance.

We do not sell your information. No mobile information, including phone numbers and SMS consent, will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent are never shared with anyone.

How We Share Information

We may share your information with insurance carriers, financial institutions, and service providers only as needed to deliver the products and services you request, or as required by law. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for third-party marketing.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You may opt out of text messages by replying STOP, and you may ask us to update or delete your information by contacting us. You may also unsubscribe from emails using the link in those messages.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above shows when it was last revised.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at stevenson@pacificridgewayinsurance.com.
