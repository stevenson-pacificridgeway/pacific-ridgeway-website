Frequently Asked Questions

Does it cost anything to get a quote or consultation?
No. Quotes and consultations are always free and come with no obligation — we're here to help you understand your options.
What does "independent" mean?
We're not tied to a single insurance company. We compare options across many carriers to find the coverage and pricing that best fit your needs.

Our network of 50+ licensed agents serves families nationwide. Product availability can vary by state, and we'll let you know what's available to you.
